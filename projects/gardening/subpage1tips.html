It is currently fall! Here are some tips on what to grow in fall and how to prepare for winter/spring!

Vegetables that Grow in Fall:

  • Lettuce
  • Cabbage
  • Kale
  • Parsley
  • Arugula
  • Spinach
  • Broccoli
  • Brussel sprouts
  • Leeks
  • Turnips
  • Beets
  • Radishes
  • Peas

Preparing Your Garden for Winter/Spring:

  • Plant garlic for harvest next year - make sure to plant before the ground freezes
  • Pull out tender vegetables, such as tomatoes and zucchini, but leave the hardy vegetables, such as broccoli and spinach, in the ground - they can handle colder weather just fine!
  • Cover the garden beds. Here are several options:
    • Add compost to the garden and cover with straw
    • Sow cover crops
    • Cover with black plastic or cardboard
  • Prune berry bushes
  • Turn off the watering hose!
